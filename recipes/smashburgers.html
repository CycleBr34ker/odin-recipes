Smash Burgers

delicous looking smash burger two patties with cheese

For my entire life i thought the best burger in the world would certainly have to be cooked on a grill. Be it gas, charcoal, or pellet. I was convinced it had to be on a grill. But damn was i wrong

The best burger is actually made in a pan/on a griddle. They are smashed. i know it sounds fucking crazy youve always heard dont smah your patties youll make them dry. well its a fucking lie.

Ingrediants

  • a big ass spatula like you use in habachi on a griddle.
  • another spatula or wooden stick thing
  • half pound of the fattiest ground beef you can find per burger
  • Kraft american cheese 2 slices per burger
  • salt
  • pepper
  • MSG

Directions

  1. get your pan or griddle smokin hot
  2. while the pan is heating up divide your half pound of ground beef into 2 even balls and season each
  3. once the pan is hot put one ball in and smash dat bitch with the big ass spatula using the stick to help apple even preasure. count to 10 then remove them the patty should stay in the pan
  4. watch the patty for the top of it to start sweating shouldnt take more than 60 seconds
  5. once its sweating flip it and continue to cook for another minute adding 1 slice of cheese after 40 seconds
  6. repeat for second patty add them hoes to toasted buns and enjoy
